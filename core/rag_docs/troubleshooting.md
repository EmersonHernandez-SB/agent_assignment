# Troubleshooting Common Issues

## Login and Access

**I forgot my password.**
1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset email (check spam if not received within 5 minutes).
4. Click the link in the email — it expires in 24 hours.
5. Enter and confirm your new password.

**I am not receiving the password reset email.**
- Verify the email address is correct and is associated with an EmerClinic account.
- Check your spam/junk folder.
- If using a work email, ask your IT admin if external emails are being blocked.
- Contact support if the problem persists.

**My account is locked.**
Accounts are locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts. Wait 15 minutes and try again, or use the Forgot Password flow to reset your credentials.

**A staff member cannot log in after being added.**
- Confirm the invite email was sent: go to **Settings → Team → Users** and check their status.
- If status is "Invite Pending", resend the invite by clicking **Resend Invite**.
- If status is "Active" but they still cannot log in, have them use Forgot Password.

---

## Scheduling Issues

**Appointment slots are not showing for a provider.**
- Confirm the provider is set as "Available": go to **Settings → Team → Providers** and check their status.
- Verify their working hours are configured under **Manage Availability**.
- Check if there is a time-off block covering the date in question.

**A patient is not appearing in the scheduling search.**
- The patient may be deactivated. Go to **Patients → Inactive Patients** to find and reactivate them.

**Calendar sync stopped working.**
- See the Calendar Sync troubleshooting section in the Calendar Sync guide.

---

## Billing Issues

**Invoice shows incorrect amount.**
- The amount may reflect a prorated charge from a mid-cycle plan change.
- If you believe it is a system error, contact billing support with the invoice ID.

**Payment method update not saving.**
- Try a different browser or clear your browser cache.
- Ensure the billing address entered matches what your bank has on file.
- If the card is being declined, contact your bank to authorize the charge.

---

## Data and Export Issues

**Export button is greyed out.**
- You may not have Export permissions. Contact your account admin.

**CSV file opens with garbled characters.**
- Open via Excel's import wizard (**Data → From Text/CSV**) and select **UTF-8** encoding.

---

## Performance Issues

**EmerClinic is loading slowly.**
- Check your internet connection speed.
- Clear your browser cache and cookies.
- Try a different browser (EmerClinic supports Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari).
- If the issue is system-wide, check the EmerClinic status page at status.emerclinic.com.

## Contacting Support

- **Basic plan:** Email support@emerclinic.com — response within 48 business hours.
- **Premium plan:** Email support@emerclinic.com or call 1-800-EMERCLINIC — response within 4 business hours.
- When contacting support, include your account ID, a description of the issue, and any screenshots or error messages.
