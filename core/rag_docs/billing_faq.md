# Billing FAQ

## When am I charged?

- **Monthly plans:** Charged on the same date each month as your sign-up date.
- **Annual plans:** Charged once per year on your renewal date.
- If a charge falls on a weekend or holiday, it is processed the next business day.

## What payment methods are accepted?

EmerClinic accepts:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- ACH/bank transfer (annual plans only)
- No cash, checks, or PayPal accepted.

## How do I view my invoices?

1. Log in to EmerClinic.
2. Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoices**.
3. All invoices are listed with date, amount, and status (Paid / Unpaid / Overdue).
4. Click any invoice to download a PDF copy.

## Why was I charged twice?

Duplicate charges can happen if:
- A plan upgrade was processed mid-cycle (you are charged the prorated amount for the new plan plus the remaining balance of the old plan).
- A payment failed and was retried automatically.

If you believe you were incorrectly charged twice, contact support with both invoice IDs. We will investigate and issue a refund within 5–7 business days if a duplicate is confirmed.

## What happens if my payment fails?

1. EmerClinic retries the charge after 3 days.
2. If the retry fails, a second retry happens after 7 days.
3. After two failed retries, the account is suspended.
4. You will receive email notifications at each step.
5. To reactivate, go to **Settings → Billing** and update your payment method.

## How do I update my payment method?

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.
2. Click **Add New Card** or **Replace Card**.
3. Enter your new card details and click Save.
4. The new card will be used for all future charges immediately.

## Can I get a refund?

- **Monthly plans:** We do not offer refunds for partial months.
- **Annual plans:** Refunds are prorated for unused full months. For example, if you cancel 4 months into an annual plan, you are eligible for a refund of the remaining 8 months.
- Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the charge.
- To request a refund, contact support at support@clinicos.com with your invoice ID and reason.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan**.
2. Select your cancellation reason.
3. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing cycle.
4. All data is retained for 90 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted.
5. You can reactivate at any time within the 90-day retention window.

## What is the late payment policy?

Invoices overdue by more than 14 days will result in account suspension. A $15 late fee is added to accounts overdue by more than 30 days. To dispute a late fee, contact billing support.
