# Appointment Scheduling Guide

## Booking a New Appointment

1. Go to **Scheduling → New Appointment** or click any open slot in the calendar view.
2. Search for the patient by name or ID. If the patient does not exist yet, click **+ Add New Patient**.
3. Select the provider from the dropdown.
4. Select the appointment type (e.g., Routine Checkup, Cleaning, Consultation, Follow-up).
5. Choose the date and time from the available slots shown.
6. Add a reason for visit (optional but recommended for provider prep).
7. Click **Confirm Appointment**.

The patient will automatically receive a confirmation email and SMS reminder (if contact info is on file).

## Viewing the Schedule

- **Day view:** Shows all appointments for all providers on a single day side by side.
- **Week view:** Shows the full week per provider.
- **Provider view:** Filter by a single provider to see only their calendar.
- **List view:** Shows appointments as a sortable/filterable table.

Switch views using the buttons in the top-right of the Scheduling screen.

## Cancelling an Appointment

1. Click on the appointment in the calendar.
2. Click **Cancel Appointment**.
3. Select a cancellation reason (Patient Request, Provider Unavailable, No-Show, Other).
4. Choose whether to notify the patient by email/SMS.
5. Confirm.

Cancelled appointments are moved to the **Cancelled** tab and are not deleted.

## Rescheduling an Appointment

1. Click on the appointment in the calendar.
2. Click **Reschedule**.
3. Select a new date, time, and optionally a different provider.
4. Click **Confirm Reschedule**.

The patient will receive an updated confirmation if notifications are enabled.

## Appointment Statuses

| Status      | Meaning                                               |
|-------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| Scheduled   | Confirmed future appointment                          |
| Confirmed   | Patient has confirmed attendance (via reminder reply) |
| Checked In  | Patient has arrived at the clinic                     |
| Completed   | Appointment finished                                  |
| Cancelled   | Cancelled by patient or clinic                        |
| No-Show     | Patient did not attend and did not cancel             |

## Automated Reminders

EmerClinic sends automatic reminders to patients:
- **48 hours before** the appointment: email reminder.
- **2 hours before** the appointment: SMS reminder (Premium plan only).

To adjust reminder settings: go to **Settings → Scheduling → Reminders**.

## Blocking Provider Time

To block time on a provider's calendar (e.g., vacation, training, lunch):

1. Go to **Scheduling → Block Time**.
2. Select the provider.
3. Set the start and end date/time.
4. Add a label (e.g., "Vacation", "Lunch Break").
5. Click **Save Block**.

Blocked times will not appear as available slots when booking.

## No-Show Management

When a patient does not show up:
1. Find the appointment and click **Mark as No-Show**.
2. EmerClinic tracks no-show rates per patient in their profile.
3. The **Reports → No-Show Report** shows clinic-wide no-show trends.
