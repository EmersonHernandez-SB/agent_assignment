# Reports and Analytics

## Overview

EmerClinic provides built-in reports to help clinic owners and managers track performance, revenue, and patient activity. Basic reports are available on all plans. Advanced analytics and custom reports require **Premium**.

## Available Reports

### Basic Plan Reports

**Daily Appointment Summary**
- Lists all appointments for a selected day.
- Shows patient name, provider, time, status, and reason for visit.
- Access: **Reports → Daily Summary**.

**Revenue Summary**
- Total invoiced amount, total collected, and outstanding balance for a date range.
- Breakdown by payment status (Paid, Unpaid, Overdue).
- Access: **Reports → Revenue Summary**.

**Patient Visit History**
- Full appointment history for an individual patient.
- Access: **Patients → select patient → Visit History**.

### Premium Plan Reports

**Provider Performance Report**
- Appointments completed, no-show rate, and revenue generated per provider.
- Filter by date range and provider.
- Access: **Reports → Provider Performance**.

**No-Show and Cancellation Report**
- No-show rate and cancellation rate by provider, day of week, and appointment type.
- Helps identify patterns for scheduling optimization.
- Access: **Reports → No-Show & Cancellations**.

**Insurance Claims Report**
- Claims by insurance provider, denial rate, and average reimbursement.
- Access: **Reports → Insurance**.

**Patient Retention Report**
- Tracks returning vs. new patients over time.
- Identifies patients who have not had a visit in over 6, 12, or 18 months.
- Access: **Reports → Patient Retention**.

**Custom Report Builder**
- Build reports from any combination of data fields.
- Save and schedule reports to be emailed automatically (daily, weekly, monthly).
- Access: **Reports → Custom Reports → New Report**.

## Exporting Reports

All reports can be exported as:
- **CSV** — for use in Excel or Google Sheets.
- **PDF** — for printing or sharing.

Click **Export** in the top-right corner of any report view.

## Scheduled Report Emails (Premium only)

1. Open any report.
2. Click **Schedule Report**.
3. Set the frequency (daily, weekly, monthly) and the time.
4. Enter the email addresses to receive the report.
5. Click **Save Schedule**.

## Key Metrics Explained

| Metric               | Definition                                                                 |
|----------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| No-show rate         | % of scheduled appointments where the patient did not attend or cancel     |
| Collection rate      | % of invoiced amount that has been paid                                    |
| Patient retention    | % of patients with a return visit within the past 12 months                |
| Revenue per provider | Total amount invoiced for appointments completed by a specific provider    |
