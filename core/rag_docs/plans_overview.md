# EmerClinic Plans Overview

## Available Plans

### Basic Plan — $99/month or $950/year
The Basic plan is designed for small clinics with a single provider.

**Included features:**
- Up to 1 provider account
- Patient records management (up to 500 active patients)
- Appointment scheduling (calendar view, reminders)
- Basic billing: invoice generation and payment tracking
- Email support (response within 48 hours)
- Standard reports: daily appointments, revenue summary
- Data export: CSV export for appointments and invoices

**Not included in Basic:**
- Multi-provider support (requires Premium)
- Insurance verification and coverage checks
- Advanced analytics and custom reports
- Calendar sync (Google Calendar, Outlook)
- Priority support
- API access
- Bulk patient import

### Premium Plan — $249/month or $2,390/year
The Premium plan is designed for multi-provider clinics and growing practices.

**Included features (everything in Basic, plus):**
- Unlimited provider accounts
- Unlimited active patients
- Insurance policy management and coverage verification
- Advanced analytics: no-show rates, revenue by provider, patient retention
- Calendar sync with Google Calendar and Outlook
- Bulk patient import (CSV upload)
- Priority support (response within 4 hours, phone support available)
- API access for custom integrations
- Custom report builder
- Automated appointment reminders (SMS + email)
- Multi-location support

## Billing Cycles

- **Monthly:** Charged on the same day each month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
- **Annual:** Billed once per year. Saves approximately 20% compared to monthly billing. Refunds are prorated for unused months.

## Upgrading or Downgrading

- Upgrades take effect immediately. You are charged the prorated difference for the remainder of the billing cycle.
- Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Your current features remain active until then.
- To change your plan, go to **Settings → Subscription → Change Plan**, or contact support.

## Free Trial

New accounts receive a 14-day free trial of the Premium plan. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, the account automatically switches to Basic unless a plan is selected.
