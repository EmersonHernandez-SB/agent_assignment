# How to Add a New Provider

Providers are the doctors, dentists, hygienists, or any clinical staff who appear in the scheduling calendar and are assigned to appointments.

> **Note:** Adding providers beyond the first one requires the **Premium plan**. Basic plan accounts are limited to 1 provider.

## Adding a Provider

1. Go to **Settings → Team → Providers**.
2. Click **Add New Provider**.
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - Full Name
   - Specialty (e.g., General Dentistry, Orthodontics, Family Medicine)
   - Role (Doctor, Hygienist, Nurse Practitioner, etc.)
4. Fill in optional fields:
   - License number
   - Email address (used for calendar sync and notifications)
   - Working hours (default schedule)
   - Color label (used to distinguish providers in the calendar)
5. Click **Save Provider**.

The provider will immediately appear in the scheduling calendar and can be assigned to appointments.

## Setting Provider Availability

1. Go to **Settings → Team → Providers** and click the provider's name.
2. Click **Manage Availability**.
3. Set their weekly working hours (e.g., Monday–Friday 8am–5pm).
4. Add exceptions for vacations or special hours using the **Block Time** feature.
5. Click **Save**.

Appointment slots for this provider will only be shown during their available hours.

## Deactivating a Provider

When a provider leaves the clinic, deactivate them instead of deleting to preserve historical appointment and billing records.

1. Go to **Settings → Team → Providers**.
2. Click the provider's name.
3. Click **Actions → Deactivate Provider**.
4. Choose what to do with their upcoming appointments:
   - **Reassign automatically** (assign to another provider you select)
   - **Leave unassigned** (you will reassign them manually)
5. Confirm the action.

## Notes

- Providers cannot be permanently deleted if they have associated appointments or medical records.
- If you need to transfer all of a provider's patients to another provider, contact EmerClinic support.
